Early Lung Cancer Detection Using Deep Learning and Biomarker

Overview

This project introduces an AI-powered system for lung cancer detection and treatment, combining medical imaging, biomarker analysis, and genetic profiling to improve diagnosis accuracy.

Challenge

Lung cancer remains one of the deadliest forms of cancer due to late detection. Traditional screening methods often miss early-stage tumors or produce false positives, leading to delayed treatment or unnecessary procedures.

Solution

Our system employs deep learning models like CNNs and SVMs to analyze medical scans with unprecedented accuracy. By combining imaging data with biomarker analysis, we can detect cancer at much earlier stages than conventional methods.

Key Features

  • Multi-modal Analysis: Combines CT scans, X-rays, and biomarker data
  • Early Detection: Identifies cancer signatures at Stage I with 94% accuracy
  • Treatment Recommendation: AI-assisted therapy suggestions based on patient data
  • Longitudinal Monitoring: Tracks disease progression over time
  • Privacy-Preserving: Uses federated learning to maintain data privacy

Technology Stack

  • Convolutional Neural Networks for image analysis
  • Support Vector Machines for classification
  • Biomarker analysis algorithms
  • Federated learning framework
  • HIPAA-compliant data storage

Impact

This system has the potential to dramatically improve lung cancer survival rates by enabling earlier detection and more personalized treatment approaches, making advanced cancer detection more accessible to healthcare providers worldwide.

Solar Powered Cold Storage with Brine Circulation and PCM Storage System

Overview

This solar-powered cold storage system provides an innovative solution for storing perishable goods in regions where electricity is limited or unreliable.

Challenge

In many rural and developing regions, lack of reliable electricity prevents proper cold storage of agricultural produce and other perishables, leading to significant food waste and economic losses.

  • Brine Circulation: Efficiently distributes cooling to storage rooms
  • IoT Monitoring: Smart sensors track temperature and performance
  • Demand-Based Cooling: Adjusts cooling based on content requirements
  • Modular Design: Scalable to different capacity needs

NanoGold: Sustainable Gold Recovery from E-Waste Using Nanotechnology

Overview

NanoGold leverages advanced nanotechnology and biological processes to extract gold from electronic waste with minimal environmental impact.

Challenge

Conventional gold recovery methods from e-waste use harmful chemicals like cyanide and mercury, creating significant environmental pollution and health hazards while achieving relatively low recovery rates.

Solution

Our technology uses specialized nanoparticles and biological processes to selectively bind to gold in e-waste, enabling efficient extraction with minimal environmental impact.

Key Features

  • Nanoparticle Technology: Selective binding to precious metals
  • Bioleaching: Microbial techniques for metal extraction
  • Plasma Arc Systems: Clean processing of complex materials
  • Closed-Loop Process: Minimal waste generation
  • High Recovery Rate: 95%+ extraction efficiency
